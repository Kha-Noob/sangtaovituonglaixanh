We dedicate a special section to express our deep gratitude to all customers, partners, and employees who have accompanied FPT throughout the 25-year journey. Success stories, congratulatory messages, and heartfelt sharing from them will be featured, painting a comprehensive picture of this invaluable cooperation and bond.

On this anniversary, FPT also reaffirms its commitment to environmental protection and sustainable development. We introduce green programs and initiatives that FPT has implemented, from waste reduction and energy saving to afforestation and the use of green technology. Articles, tips on environmental protection, and information about volunteer activities will help you easily participate and contribute to these efforts.

" Celebrating 25 Years of Innovation and Sustainability: Join Us in Protecting Our Planet for Future Generations ! "

The website will continuously update information about FPT's 25th anniversary activities, including online and offline events, competitions, and customer appreciation programs. You can register to participate, follow the events, and share your memorable moments with the FPT community.

A rich resource library with articles, videos, and documents provides detailed information about FPT's history, success stories, and environmental protection initiatives. This is a valuable resource for anyone wanting to learn more about FPT and our activities.
Join us in celebrating FPT's 25th anniversary and working together to build a greener, more sustainable future. Every small action you take contributes to significant changes for the environment and the community. Thank you for accompanying FPT on this meaningful journey!
