Chairman Truong Gia Binh: ‘Betting’ the Future of FPT on AI

    

 According to FPT Chairman Truong Gia Binh, the artificial intelligence development strategy is closely tied to FPT's destiny. This is an important historical turning point for FPT to continue making significant strides in the future.

Previously, at the FPT Annual General Meeting of Shareholders 2024 held in April, Truong Gia Binh mentioned five keywords that would determine the course of human history: “Tuệ, Bán, Xe, Số, Xanh”, representing Artificial Intelligence (AI), Semiconductors, Electric Vehicles, Digital Transformation, and Green Transformation. He believes that these five keywords have shaped human history for the past three-quarters of a century and will continue to do so for the remaining quarter-century.

     Mr. Truong Gia Binh at the conference "Future of FPT AI" held at Hola Park 2, Hoa Lac, Hanoi, marking an important turning point in the development strategy of FPT Corporation.

     Recently, in the in-depth discussion “Future of FPT AI” with the participation of FPT's executive board, leaders of subsidiaries, and leading AI experts from the corporation, Chairman Truong Gia Binh shared insights about FPT's future direction and strategy related to AI development. According to Truong Gia Binh, all five keywords “Tuệ, Bán, Xe, Số, Xanh” are related to AI - artificial intelligence. FPT will invest all resources into this largest "bet" in its history, aiming to become a leading enterprise in AI.

Especially about ‘Green’: Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Environmental Protection at FPT

     The application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in environmental protection activities is becoming increasingly important and effective. At FPT, AI is used in various fields to minimize negative environmental impacts and promote sustainable development.

Monitoring and Analyzing Environmental Data

     AI is used to analyze environmental data from various sources such as sensors, satellites, and IoT (Internet of Things) systems. AI algorithms help predict climate trends, detect abnormal fluctuations, and provide early warnings about natural disasters. As a result, preventive and response measures can be implemented promptly and effectively.

Optimizing Production Processes

      FPT uses AI to optimize production processes, minimizing energy and material consumption, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. For example, in manufacturing plants, AI can help adjust processes to save electricity and water while increasing material utilization efficiency.

Environmental Protection Policies and Commitments

     FPT has issued environmental policies aimed at reducing energy consumption, reducing the use of harmful chemicals, and promoting recycling. FPT's goal is to cut total greenhouse gas emissions by 15.8% by 2030 and achieve Net Zero emissions by 2040.
