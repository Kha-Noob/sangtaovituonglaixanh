The second period from 2010 to 2019

Affirming FPT's Position and Achievements

With over three decades of operation in the IT industry, FPT has established itself as a leading corporation in Vietnam and a reputable technology developer globally. Since its inception, FPT has been relentless in innovation and expanding its operational scope, delivering effective technology solutions to thousands of clients both domestically and internationally.

FPT has built a community of talented and capable experts while nurturing and developing multiple generations of IT professionals for Vietnam and the region. Particularly noteworthy is the establishment of FPT University, a pivotal move that has provided high-quality human resources for the technology sector, meeting the increasing demands of the labor market.

Furthermore, FPT has been at the forefront of applying cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), big data analytics, and cybersecurity solutions, enabling businesses and organizations to undergo digital transformations effectively.

With a steadfast commitment to social responsibility and sustainable development, FPT continues to make positive contributions to the community, from educational activities to environmental protection projects and community support programs.

From its early steps to the present day, FPT has not only achieved remarkable milestones but also solidified its position and mission in advancing the technology industry, contributing to global economic and social development.

2010: Expansion into Digital Services
FPT expanded its focus to include digital services, leveraging its expertise in software and IT solutions to offer comprehensive digital transformation services to clients.

2011: Innovation and Technology Leadership
FPT continued to innovate and strengthen its position as a technology leader, focusing on emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), big data analytics, and cybersecurity solutions.

2012: Strategic Acquisitions and Partnerships
FPT strategically acquired and formed partnerships with technology firms globally, enhancing its capabilities and expanding its market reach across various industries.

2013: Launch of FPT Software
FPT Software was launched as a subsidiary specializing in providing software outsourcing and IT services to international clients, further solidifying FPT's global presence.

2014: Sustainable Development Initiatives
FPT intensified its commitment to sustainable development, launching initiatives aimed at reducing environmental impact and promoting corporate social responsibility (CSR).

2015: Focus on Smart Cities and IoT
FPT began focusing on smart city solutions and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies, contributing to urban development projects and digital transformation initiatives

2016: Digital Transformation Acceleration
FPT accelerated its digital transformation efforts, helping businesses across sectors embrace digital technologies to improve efficiency and competitiveness.

2017: Expansion of AI and Machine Learning
FPT expanded its capabilities in artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning, developing AI-powered solutions for diverse applications in industries such as finance, healthcare, and manufacturing.

2018: Innovation Hubs and Tech Incubators
FPT established innovation hubs and tech incubators to foster entrepreneurship and support startups, encouraging innovation and collaboration within the tech ecosystem.

2019: Global Recognition and Expansion
By 2019, FPT gained global recognition for its technological advancements and expanded its operations to new markets, solidifying its position as a key player in the global technology landscape.
