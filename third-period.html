The Third period from 2020 to 2024

Leading the Way in Digital Transformation and Innovation

Since its inception, FPT has been at the forefront of driving digital transformation and innovation across industries. With a legacy spanning over three decades, FPT has evolved into a prominent technology corporation, known for its pioneering solutions and commitment to excellence. From its humble beginnings to its current global stature, FPT has continually embraced emerging technologies, from artificial intelligence to blockchain, to empower businesses and communities worldwide.

Throughout the years, FPT has built a robust ecosystem of digital services, extending its reach to various sectors including finance, healthcare, education, and telecommunications. By consistently investing in research and development, FPT has introduced cutting-edge solutions that address complex challenges, enhance operational efficiency, and deliver superior customer experiences.

One of the key pillars of FPT's success is its unwavering dedication to talent development. FPT University, established in 2006, has become a cornerstone in nurturing a new generation of IT professionals who are well-equipped to tackle the demands of a rapidly evolving digital landscape. The institution's innovative curriculum and hands-on approach have produced many of the leading experts in Vietnam’s tech industry.

As a trusted partner for digital transformation, FPT has forged strategic alliances with leading global technology firms, expanding its capabilities and market presence. These partnerships have enabled FPT to deliver comprehensive, end-to-end solutions that drive innovation and growth for clients around the world.

Looking ahead, FPT remains focused on its vision of a digital future where technology serves as a catalyst for progress and well-being. The company's ongoing investments in AI, big data, cloud computing, and other transformative technologies position it to continue leading the charge in the digital age. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, sustainability, and community, FPT is poised to make a lasting impact on the global technology landscape.

Despite global uncertainties, FPT continued its focus on innovation, launching new initiatives in AI-driven technologies, IoT solutions, and blockchain applications. In 2022, FPT expanded its global footprint through strategic partnerships and acquisitions, enhancing its capabilities in emerging technologies and market segments. FPT scaled its impact through transformative projects across industries, focusing on enhancing operational efficiency, customer experience, and sustainability. As FPT looks towards the future in 2024, it continues to innovate and lead in the digital age.
